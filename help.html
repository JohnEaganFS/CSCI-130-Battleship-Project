How to start a game:
To begin a game, first make sure you have created an account and logged in.
Next, go the "Play" page and invite another player or accept an invite sent to you.
Finally, join the active game by selecting it in the "Active Games" menu.

How to Play Battleship:
Battleship consists of two players, each with their own board.
When you first join a game, you will be able to place your ships on your board.
You will place:
1. Carrier (Size = 5)
2. Battleship (Size = 4)
3. Destroyer (Size = 3)
4. Submarine (Size = 3)
5. Patrol Boat (Size = 2)

After placing your ships, you will press "Ready" and wait for the other player to place their ships and ready up.
Once both players are ready, you will rejoin the game and begin the game.

Once a game has begun, the players alternate turns of firing torpedos at the opponent's board.
The goal is to hit and destroy all of the opponent's ships.
When one player loses all their ships, the game will end and the player with ships remaining wins.
